About Us

We are the world's largest university

We are the world's largest university, with over 150,000 students enrolled in more than 1,000 academic programs across all levels of study. Our students come from all over the globe to pursue their dreams in our classrooms, laboratories, and libraries. Our faculty are renowned in their fields, and our alumni have gone on to become leaders in their professions. We are proud of what we have achieved so far and striving to do far better in future.
